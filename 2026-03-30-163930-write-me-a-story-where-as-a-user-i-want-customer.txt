вҸә User Story: External OVF Link & Salesforce Record Creation

  ---
  Story Title

  As a Sales/CSM user, I want to send customers a link to fill out an OVF 
  externally, so that their responses are automatically captured as a Salesforce
   record.

  ---
  User Story

  в–Һ As a Sales Representative or Customer Success Manager,
  в–Һ I want to generate and share a unique OVF (Opportunity Verification Form) 
  link with my customer,
  в–Һ So that the customer can fill in required information at their convenience, 
  and the submitted data is automatically stored as a record in Salesforce вҖ” 
  without manual data entry on my end.

  ---
  Acceptance Criteria

  Given I am on a Salesforce record (e.g., Opportunity or Account),
  When I click "Generate OVF Link",
  Then a unique, time-bound external form URL is generated and copied/sent to
  the customer.

  ---
  Given the customer receives the OVF link,
  When they open it in any browser (no Salesforce login required),
  Then they see a branded form with the required fields to fill out.

  ---
  Given the customer completes and submits the OVF form,
  When submission is confirmed,
  Then:
  - A new Salesforce record (e.g., OVF_Response__c) is created automatically
  - The record is linked back to the originating Opportunity/Account
  - The submitting user (Sales Rep / CSM) receives an in-app or email
  notification
  - The OVF link is marked as "Submitted" and can no longer be reused

  ---
  Given the OVF link expires before submission,
  When the customer attempts to open it,
  Then they see a friendly expiry message and are prompted to contact their rep.

  ---
  Out of Scope

  - Customer authentication / Salesforce login
  - Manual data entry by the rep after customer submission
  - Editing a submitted OVF

  ---
  Notes / Open Questions

  1. What object does the OVF record link to вҖ” Opportunity, Account, or a custom
   object?
  2. What fields are on the OVF form (customer-facing)?
  3. What is the link expiry window (e.g., 7 days, 30 days)?
  4. Is the external form hosted via Salesforce Experience Cloud, a third-party
  form tool, or a custom web app?
  5. Should the customer receive a confirmation email upon submission?

  ---
  Shall I kick off the 7-agent Salesforce workflow to design and build this
  feature? If yes, please clarify the open questions above (especially Q1 and
  Q4) so the design agent can produce an accurate implementation plan.
